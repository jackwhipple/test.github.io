some of my inspirations

Nathan Fielder

I love Nathan Fielder! I love Nathan for You, On Your Side, and his YouTube stuff. I was definitely inspired by how he utilizes random people from Craigslist. I also think he's super hot. 

Oh No, Ross and Carrie

Oh No, Ross and Carrie is a podcast that I really enjoy. It's gotten me into learning about some alternative medicines and fringe spiritual groups. I love looking online at alternative medicine websites, like watersmacker.com. 

I also love the hosts so much!

Megan Stalter

Megan Stalter is a comedian on Instagram and Twitter. I love her soo much. I think she's so silly and funny without appropriation or mean-spiritedness. Hubol showed her to me, and I've loved keeping up with her since.

Eric Andre

Hubol showed me Eric Andre a long time ago. I love his pranks so much.

Hubol

Hubol is my brother, so that's pretty awesome. He makes really cool art, and he's super encouraging about my art. I love his range, like some of his art is so raw and other stuff is more polished. <3

On Cinema at the Cinema

I love the silliness of this show! Hubol introduced me to this show, and it's super comforting to me. I love the trial, and I think that's the best place to start. 

Patti Harrison

I think Patti Harrison is so funny. She's so cool. There used to be some series on Youtube that she did for SeriouslyTV, but the channel was deleted, and now there are some still remaining on their YouTube page. I think I first found her googling "trans comedians" and watched all her stuff. It was so cool to see her in Shrill.

The Couple in the Cage: Guatinaui Odyssey by Coco Fusco and Paula Heredia

I learned about this piece in an art class, and I fell in love with it. The misinterpretations and interactive elements were super inspiring. I loved reading one of the creator's notes about the piece, like how the museum curators were horrified when they saw that people believed it. If you haven't read about this piece, I'd definitely recommend learning about it! This writing on the performance is amazing, but I think you need access via a university or something like that to read it. If you'd like to read it feel free to send me an email.

Dr. Gameshow

This podcast is so sweet and silly. I love that Manolo is so terrible at all his responsibilities. I love it when people mess up the game rules, or when the kids say stuff that's like whattttt.

My Calling (Card) #1 by Adrian Piper

I learned about this piece in that same art class. I love to think about what the person who received it does after, like if they put it somewhere or throw it away.
